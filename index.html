Welcome

I am a materials chemist specializing in polyoxometalates (POMs), selenium-based clusters, and NDI-macrocycles for high-performance organic photovoltaics (OPV). My work aims to engineer superior electron transport layers (ETLs) for improved power conversion efficiency and stability.

Explore My Research

Research Interests

Se-POM ETLs

Development of selenotungstate clusters like [W₁₈O₅₄(SeO₃)₂]ⁿ⁻ for tunable work functions and interfacial dipoles in OPV devices.

NDI Macrocycles

Rigid triangular NDI-Δ systems for supramolecular charge transport and single-component D-A hybrids.

POM-Organic Hybrids

Covalent and coordination hybrids for novel interfacial materials with enhanced redox properties.
